Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What services does TrustVest Capital offer?

TrustVest Capital provides a range of investment and wealth management solutions, including portfolio management, trading platforms, and financial advisory services tailored to individual and institutional investors.

2. Is my money safe with TrustVest Capital?

Yes. Client funds are held in segregated accounts with regulated financial institutions. We follow strict compliance and regulatory standards to ensure your money is protected.

3. How can I open an account?

Opening an account is easy. Visit our website and click on “Open Account”. You’ll need to complete a registration form and provide identification documents in line with KYC (Know Your Customer) requirements.

4. What documents are required for verification?

Typically, we require a valid government-issued ID (passport or driver’s license) and a recent proof of address (such as a utility bill or bank statement dated within the last 3 months).

5. How do I deposit and withdraw funds?

You can deposit funds via bank transfer or other accepted payment methods listed in your account portal. Withdrawals can be requested through the same portal and are typically processed within 1–3 business days.

6. Are there any fees or charges?

We strive to be transparent with our fees. Charges may apply for certain services such as account inactivity, withdrawals, or advisory management. Please refer to our Fee Schedule for full details.

7. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team by emailing support@trustvestcapital.com or calling us at +44 (0)1234 567890. Our team is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm (GMT).

8. Where is TrustVest Capital regulated?

TrustVest Capital operates in accordance with applicable financial regulatory bodies. Detailed regulatory and licensing information can be found on our website under the “Regulatory Disclosures” section.
