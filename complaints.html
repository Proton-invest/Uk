Complaints Handling Procedure

At TrustVest Capital, we are committed to providing the highest standards of service to our clients. However, we understand that there may be occasions when our service does not meet expectations. This document outlines how you can raise a complaint and how we will respond.

1. How to Raise a Complaint

If you are dissatisfied with any aspect of our service, you can submit a complaint using any of the following methods:

  • Email: complaints@trustvestcapital.com
  • Phone: +44 (0)1234 567890
  • Post: TrustVest Capital, 123 Finance Street, London, UK

2. What Information to Include

To help us handle your complaint efficiently, please include the following information:

  • Your full name and contact details
  • Details of the complaint and relevant dates
  • Any supporting documents or evidence
  • What you would like us to do to resolve the issue

3. Our Complaints Process

  • We will acknowledge your complaint within 3 business days.
  • Your complaint will be investigated thoroughly and fairly.
  • We aim to resolve all complaints within 15 business days. If more time is needed, we will inform you in writing.
  • Once a decision is reached, we will notify you in writing with a full explanation.

4. Escalation

If you are not satisfied with our final response, you may refer your complaint to an independent dispute resolution body or relevant financial ombudsman, depending on your jurisdiction.

5. Commitment to Improvement

All complaints are reviewed by our compliance team to identify opportunities for service improvement and staff training.
